As a nation that has a very proud history Sri Lankans have tried various kind of foods.So Sri Lankans are very rich by traditional foods that inherited from ancestors.There For when we try to make a recipe catalog for Sri Lankan foods it will need hundreds of pages.So here i'll give you short recipe catalog of Sri Lankan foods here.

Here, Let's concider recipes under four categories :

1.Mains

Chicken Curry

Sri Lankan chicken curry is a flavorful and aromatic dish that reflects the rich culinary heritage of Sri Lanka. It is a popular and staple dish in Sri Lankan cuisine, known for its combination of spices and coconut milk.

Dhal Curry

Sri Lankan dhal curry, also known as parippu curry, is a delicious and nutritious dish that holds a significant place in Sri Lankan cuisine. It is a lentil-based curry prepared using red or yellow lentils (dal), which are rich in protein and fiber

Jack Fruit Curry

Sri Lankan jackfruit curry, often known as "polos curry," is a flavorful and aromatic dish that showcases the versatility of jackfruit in Sri Lankan cuisine. Jackfruit, a large tropical fruit, is used in its unripe or green state for this curry.

2.Desserts

Aluwa

Sri Lankan Aluwa is a traditional sweet confection that holds a special place in the country's culinary traditions, especially during festive occasions and celebrations.

Mung Guli

Mung Guli is very especial traditional food that people carry when go for travels in Sri Lanka.It is very popular even in New Year seoson and it is made by using rice flour and green beans.

Konde Kaum

Konda Kaum is a must for the New year table in Sri Lanka.It is made by using rice flour,honey and there must be a good practice to make it properly.

3.Appetizers

Parippu Wade

In Sri Lankan cuisine, "wade" refers to a popular and savory deep-fried snack. These are often consumed as street food, during festive occasions, or as appetizers. Wade come in various forms, with one of the most well-known being "parippu wade," made primarily from lentils.

Cutlet

Sri Lankan cutlet is a popular snack or appetizer enjoyed throughout the country. It is a deep-fried, spiced, and breaded patty typically filled with a mixture of spiced meat and vegetables

Vegitable Roti

Sri Lankan vegetable roti is a popular and flavorful dish that combines a variety of vegetables, spices, and flatbread. It is a common street food and a favorite among locals and tourists alike

4.Beverages

Young Coconut(Kurumba)

Sri Lankan young coconut, also known as "thambili" or "kurumba," is a popular tropical fruit that is highly valued for its refreshing and hydrating properties.

Herbal Porridge

Sri Lankan herbal porridge, commonly known as "kola kenda," is a traditional dish that has both nutritional and medicinal value. It is a porridge made from a variety of herbs, spices, and grains, often consumed for its health benefits

Ceylon Tea

Sri Lankan tea, often referred to as Ceylon tea, is renowned worldwide for its exceptional quality and unique flavor profile.
