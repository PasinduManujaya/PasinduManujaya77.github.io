Individual Recipe Chicken Curry Aluwa Wade

Ingredients with quantities

  • 2 cups young jackfruit, cleaned and chopped into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 sprig curry leaves
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 2-3 cardamom pods
  • 2-3 cloves
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 tablespoon grated ginger
  • 3-4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2-3 green chilies, sliced (adjust according to spice preference)
  • 1 tablespoon red chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 tablespoon coriander powder
  • 1 teaspoon fenugreek seeds
  • 1 can (400 ml) coconut milk
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish (optional)

Cooking Instructions

1.In a large pan or pot, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat.

2.Add mustard seeds to the hot oil. When they start to pop, add cumin seeds, curry leaves, cinnamon stick, cardamom pods, cloves, and fenugreek seeds. Sauté for a minute to release the flavors.

3.Add chopped onions and sauté until they become golden brown.

4.Add minced garlic, grated ginger, and green chilies. Sauté for another couple of minutes until the raw smell disappears.

5.Add red chili powder, turmeric powder, and coriander powder. Mix well to coat the spices evenly.

6.Add chopped jackfruit to the pot and stir to combine with the spice mixture.

7.Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they become soft.

8.Pour in the coconut milk and stir well. Bring the curry to a simmer.

9.Cover the pot and let the jackfruit curry cook over low to medium heat for about 20-30 minutes or until the jackfruit is tender.

10.Adjust salt according to your taste.

11.Once the jackfruit is cooked, remove the curry from heat.

12.Garnish with fresh coriander leaves if desired.
